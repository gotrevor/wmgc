Western Massachusetts Go Club

We play Go (wei qi, baduk) every week in Western Massachusetts, mainly Amherst and Northampton. Go is a fascinating 4,000 year-old board game played by millions worldwide. We welcome newcomers, and are happy to teach the game to anyone. We travel to nearby tournaments in Boston, Connecticut, and elsewhere, and we also host tournaments of our own.

Thanks to the American Go Association for hosting our web-site.

Currently, we meet on THURSDAY nights at Rao's Coffee in Amherst, around 7pm. Please join us! See a map with directions to Rao's.

Please feel free to join our mailing list or just browse the archives!
