Fall 2003 Tournament

Format: AGA-sanctioned handicapped tournament, 4 rounds.

When: September 28th, 2003

In the 2K and higher category, first place went to Gregory Rosenblatt and second place went to Huan Yang.
In the 3K - 10K category, first place went to Li Song and second place went to Jeffrey Miller.
In the 10K and lower category, first place went to Andrew Hall and second place went the Daisuke Tanaka.
